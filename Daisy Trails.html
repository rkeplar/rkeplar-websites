Daisy on the Trails

Daisy adores the outdoors, she is always ready for a walk no matter how long she has been playing outside.

While my parents have a yard she can run all around in, I live in an apartment. Instead, I try to take her on hour long walks every day unless it is raining badly, she is scared of storms.

There are beautiful trails that run alongside the white river behind where I live. There are many access points to the water, many of which are overgrown and secluded. Daisy loves these little coves. She loves to splash in the water and roll around in the grass. She becomes a very muddy puppy very quickly. She does her runs alongside the hill and army crawls over thick vegetation.

Along the rest of the trails, she sniffs around and looks back at me and smiles as she pulls me forward. Luckily, she doesn't have any interest in wild animals so she never tries to catch or chase after anything.

She waits patiently as I take nature photos, but presses forward with the hike if she catches me taking photos of her. We quickly learned Daisy has a 7th sense for knowing if her photo is being taken and that she does not particularly care for modelling.

At the end of the hike Daisy still has the energy to run up the flights of stairs to my apartment and wait patiently for me to open the door. She'll get a few good sips of water before curling up next to me for a nap. Unless she got muddy and is need of a bath - but that is a story for another page.
